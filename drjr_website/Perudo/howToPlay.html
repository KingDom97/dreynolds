How To Play

The Rules

The aim of the game is to be the last remaining player with any dice

The game is played in rounds

Each player must place a bet on how many of a dice they think there will be between all the players

Aces (1) are wildcards, they count as any dice and so are included in the count at the end of a round

If the bet is changed to aces, the number bet must be a minimum of half of the previous bet

To end a round, a player calls "Dudo"

"Dudo" is called when a player believes that the previous bet is unlikely

If their call is correct, the player that placed the bet loses a dice

If they are wrong, the player that called "Dudo" loses a dice

When a player is reduced to one dice, the next round is palifico and this player starts the betting

In a palifico round the dice bet on remains the same and aces are no longer wildcards

When a player runs out of dice, they are out of the game
